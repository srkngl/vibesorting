Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

VibeSorting ("we", "our", or "us") operates vibesorting.vercel.app. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive from Google:

  • Your name (as set in your Google account)
  • Your email address
  • Your Google account ID (used as a unique identifier)
  • Your profile picture URL

When you use the app, we store:

  • Your rankings (which items you ranked and in what order)
  • The category and subcategory you played
  • Timestamps of your rankings

We do not collect passwords, payment information, location data, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your name and avatar in the app
  • To save and retrieve your ranking history
  • To show anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g. "47% of users ranked Messi #1")
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties beyond what is necessary to run the service.

3. Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled via Supabase using Google OAuth 2.0. We only request the minimum scopes needed: email, profile, and openid. We never receive your Google password.

You can revoke VibeSorting's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU region. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to maintain your session token between page visits. No third-party tracking cookies are used. We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising trackers.

6. Data Retention

Your account and ranking data are retained for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove all personally identifiable data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

VibeSorting is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of VibeSorting after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact
